Important — Prescription Medicines in Kenya

Under Kenya's Pharmacy & Poisons Act (Cap 244), a wide range of medicines may only be dispensed upon presentation of a valid prescription issued by a licensed medical practitioner. These include antibiotics, antihypertensives, antidiabetics, antiretrovirals, steroids, controlled substances, and many other scheduled medicines. Jack's Pharmacy Limited strictly adheres to this law. Medicines marked "Rx Required" on our catalogue cannot and will not be dispensed without a valid prescription. Please visit your doctor before coming to us for prescription medicines.

Our Story

37 Years of Trusted Care

Jack's Pharmacy Limited was founded in 1989 with a single, unwavering mission: to make genuine, quality pharmaceutical care accessible to every family in Kisii. What began as a trusted neighbourhood pharmacy has grown into one of Kisii's most recognised and relied-upon medicine suppliers.

We are a PPB-licensed pharmacy, stocking over 5,000 genuine medicines across all categories — from everyday OTC medicines to prescription pharmaceuticals, baby care, medical devices and health supplements. Every product we stock is sourced from verified, legitimate suppliers.

Under our tagline "Your Trusted Pharmacy — Caring for your Health, Everyday", we remain committed to the Kisii community. Our registered pharmacists ensure that every prescription is verified and every customer receives accurate pharmaceutical guidance.

Jack's Pharmacy Limited. Registered in Kenya. PPB Licensed. NHIF Accredited.
