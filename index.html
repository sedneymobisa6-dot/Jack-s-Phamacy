Health Education

What is a Pharmacist?

A pharmacist is a licensed health professional specialised in the science of medications — their composition, effects, safe dosing, drug interactions and correct dispensing. In Kenya, all practising pharmacists must be registered with the Pharmacy & Poisons Board (PPB) and hold a minimum of a Bachelor of Pharmacy degree.

At Jack's Pharmacy, our pharmacists do far more than dispense medicines. They review your prescriptions for safety, advise on correct usage and side effects, screen for harmful drug interactions, provide guidance on over-the-counter medications, and serve as your first point of contact for everyday health questions — helping you make informed decisions about your health and that of your family.

Pharmacy Health Tips

Expert health guidance from our pharmacists — for you and your family.

💊
Medication Safety

Always Complete Your Antibiotic Course

Stopping antibiotics early — even when you feel better — causes drug resistance. Always complete the full prescribed course.

🩸
Chronic Conditions

Managing Diabetes with Your Pharmacist

Monitor your blood sugar regularly. Bring your glucometer readings to your pharmacist — we help you understand what they mean.

👶
Child Health

When to See a Pharmacist vs. a Doctor

Mild fever, coughs and rashes can often be safely managed with pharmacy guidance. We'll tell you when to escalate to a doctor.

Serving Kisii's Health for Over Three Decades

Jack's Pharmacy opened its doors on Hospital Road, Kisii in 1988 — at a time when reliable, quality pharmaceutical care was a genuine need in the community. From day one, the founding commitment was simple: stock only original, quality medicines and treat every patient with the care and respect they deserve.

Over 37 years, that commitment has never wavered. While many pharmacies have come and gone, Jack's Pharmacy has remained a constant on Hospital Road — a trusted name that generations of Kisii families have relied upon for their health needs.

Today, we continue to serve hundreds of patients every week, maintaining the same standards of pharmaceutical excellence that have defined us since 1988. Our 5.0 Google rating and the words of our long-standing customers are the truest measure of what we have built.
