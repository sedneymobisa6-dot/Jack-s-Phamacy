Serving Kisii's Health for Over Three Decades

Jack's Pharmacy opened its doors on Hospital Road, Kisii in 1989 — at a time when reliable, quality pharmaceutical care was a genuine need in the community. From day one, the founding commitment was simple: stock only original, quality medicines and treat every patient with the care and respect they deserve.

Over 37 years, that commitment has never wavered. Jack's Pharmacy has remained a constant on Hospital Road — a trusted name that generations of Kisii families have relied upon for their health needs.

Today we have expanded into digital health services — online ordering, prescription uploads, telehealth consultations and lab test booking — bringing the same trusted care to the digital age.
